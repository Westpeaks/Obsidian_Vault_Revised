## Standards

- Enter time daily
	- On Friday, complete, review, and submit your time sheet. 
- If you do not submit your time sheet on Friday, it must be submitted by 12 PM CST the following Monday.
- Time cards cannot be submitted with zero hours. Time cards with zero hours must be removed. 
- Enter all time worked. 
- When planning PTO, submit you time sheet in advance.
