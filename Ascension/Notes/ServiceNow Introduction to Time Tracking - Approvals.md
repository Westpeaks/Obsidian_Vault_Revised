## Approvals

Time card approvals vary depending on your role. 

### AT Associates

**Project Time Cards:**
- Require approval from project manager.
- Must be approved by Monday 5:00 PM CST. 
- Time will update to processed after being approved. 

**Non-project Time Cards:**
- Require approval by the hiring manager. 

### Talent Flex/TAPFIN

**Project Time Cards**
- Approved by project manager and hiring manager.
- Must be approved by 5:00 PM CST. 

**Non-project Time Cards**

- Require approval from the hiring manager.
