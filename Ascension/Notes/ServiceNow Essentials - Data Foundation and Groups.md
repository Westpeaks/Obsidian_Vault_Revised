## Data Foundation and Groups

### What are groups and why are they important?

Groups in SNow are user who share common responsibilities and functions.

#### Importance

- They simplify task assignments, approvals, and communications
- They streamline workflows
- Enforce security by restricting access
- Support collaboration

#### Group Types

Group types define roles and functions of groups

- **ACGL** - Provide users with access to features in SNow. 
- **ACGO** - Controls access to reports, dashboards, VTBs, and tags in SNow.
- **Approval** - Supports approval workflows (requests, changes, and tasks). Ensures governance and compliance.
- **ITIL** - Responsible for resolving requests and incidents. Handle day-to-day IT service management. 
- **On-call** - Supports MIBs page outs, spifically for leaders not part of standard resolver groups. 
- **Resource** - Support resource and capacity management. Help manage and allocate resources.

**Group Managers** manage groups and their assigned work.
