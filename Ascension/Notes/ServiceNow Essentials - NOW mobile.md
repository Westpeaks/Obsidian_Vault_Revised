## NOW Mobile

### What is NOW Mobile?

It is the mobile solution to SNow (requires login).

### What is offered on NOW Mobile?

**Navigate to:**
- Home 
- Help Center
- Notifications
- Settings

#### Home

**View:**
- Quick links
- Report an issue
- My Items
- My tasks
- My requests
- My assets
- My Assessments/surveys

#### Help Center

**View:**
- Recommended help for you
- Popular topics
- Browse all topics

#### Notifications

**View:**
- You can enable and disable notifications and view them if enabled.

#### Settings

**You can:**
- Adjust notification settings
- Configure personal settings
- Manage language and accessibility
- View app information
