## Introduction

### What is ServiceNow?

**ServiceNow** (SNow) presents a unified platform for the following processes:
- Service request fulfillment through a service catalog. 
- Issue management and IT support
- Asset, service, and configuration tracking
- Data and reporting
- Strategic portfolio management

### For Ascension

We use SNow for the following:

- Deliver timely and reliable services
- Standardize and scale processes
- Automate repetitive work
- Track and decide how we invest time , talent, and resources
- Align work and resources

### User Roles in ServiceNow

- Requestor - most common role. Submits requests and incidents. 
- Fulfiller (ITIL User) - manages and completes requests. Assigns tasks and escalates when needed, 
- Approver - Review requests. Approve or rejects actions directly from ticket sources (email, MyTechnology, or SNow).
- Group Manager - Assigned to manage a support group. Tracks team workload and performance.
- Process Owner - Optimizes and manages workflows in SNow. Monitors process performance. Review and approves changes for workflows. Collaborates with IT and business teams using SNow. Tracks and resolves process incidents. 
- Product Owner - Prioritizes and manages product backlog. Defines and communicates product requirements. Engage with users and stakeholders. Monitors product usage. 
- Administrator/Platform Owner - Sets up workflows, dashboards, and integrations. Manages access and securituy. Supports upgrades and enhancements.
