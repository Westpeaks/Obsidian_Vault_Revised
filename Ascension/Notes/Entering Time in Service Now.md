## When 

- Time should be entered on Fridays. Time is entered on ServiceNow.
- Once the time has been entered, a screenshot should be sent to tekstlouispayroll@teksystems.com.

## Where

- Time is to be submitted Resources/EDU page of the AppSec Security Governance resource (bookmarked webpage). 


## Main Categories for  Time Submission 

### Administration

- Should be submitted for time spent most of the time working in Onspring. 
### Personal Development

- Used for meeting times (Admin or review meetings)
- Any general learning sessions should also be entered as this.
### Discovery
- Times where you are assessing a POC or gaining understanding of a new implementation.
