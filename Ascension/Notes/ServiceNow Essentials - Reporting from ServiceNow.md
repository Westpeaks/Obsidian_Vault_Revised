## Reporting

### Roles and Responsibilities in Reporting

#### Process Owners

- Create reports on process-related metrics
- Review operational reporting needs

#### Process-Related Analytics Team

- Develop reports based on job functions and roles
- Collaborate with reporting team

#### Technology Business Management (TBM) Team

- Develops a catalog of available reports and solutions

#### Report Creators (ITIL Role Required)

Can create and edit:
- Reports
- Dashboards
- Basic dashboard widgets
- Dashboard filters

#### Analytics Governance

- Manages publishing and scheduling requests

### Best Practices

- Search for what exists that can meet your needs.
- Save reports that you will use repeatedly.
- Leverage Service Operations Workspace, reduce the need for creating custom team dashboards.
