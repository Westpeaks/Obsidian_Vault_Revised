## AWS Regions and Availability Zones

AWS infrastructure consists of the following:

- Regions - physical locations based in a region of the world. They will consists of a group of data centers in that region.
- Availability zones - these are the group of data centers in a particular region. Each region consists of a minimum of three AZs. They are geographically separated in case of a natural disaster. 

These measures results in high availability of AWS services.
