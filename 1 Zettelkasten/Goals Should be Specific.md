It should follow three sets of criteria:

1. Which action you will take. 
2. How long you will take. 
3. How often you will do the work. 

**Ex.** I am going to save 500 dollars a month. 

**Ex.** I am going to study AWS course material two hours a day, for two months,  to get my cloud practitioner certification.
