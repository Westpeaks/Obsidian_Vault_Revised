Accountability is an important part of keeping with set goals.

How to keep yourself accountable:

- Regular review of goal or goals
- Accountability partners
- Community

## System for Accomplishing Goals

Goals can be broken up in order to be accomplished with greater proficiency. 

- Grand adventures - A high level outcome
	- Ex. Land my first position as a DevOps Engineer
- Monthly Quests - A monthly objective that should be specific.
	- Set up a homelab Kubernetes cluster running a static website.
- Weekly Objectives - Tasks to help get towards a monthly goal. 
- Daily Deeds and Practices - Tasks that are done each day to accomplish a weekly objectives. 
	- The actual work.
