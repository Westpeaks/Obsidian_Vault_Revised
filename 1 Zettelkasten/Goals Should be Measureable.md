We have to be able to quantify the goals we have and measure them. 

- Time is and overarching quantifier. You should almost always indicate the time you are going to dedicate towards achieving your goals. Your goal should have other quantifiers if you are not using time. 

	- Study for 4 hours a day. 
	- Spend two hours on a project.
	- Save and invest 500 dollars a month.
