## Lamb Marinade

Start with a jar. Portions are by eye and taste.

- 2/3 Olive oil
- 1/3 White wine
- Lemon juice
- Chicken bouillon cube or tsp of BTB.   
- Garlic 
- Rosemary 
- Thyme
- Lemon Zest
- Mustard (French Dijon or Yellow English)
- Honey 
- Salt & Pepper 
- Greek oregano

Once combined, blend.

## Instructions

1. Line the bottom of a walled tray or casserole dish with wet parchment. 
2. Pour a quarter to a third of the marinade on the bottom. 
3. Line the dish with cut potatoes (Yukon Gold is preferable). 
4. Pour another third onto potatoes. 
5. Place lamb chops on top (lamb chops should be cut as single bone chops).
6. Pour rest of marinade on top of the chops. 
7. cover with another sheet of wet parchment. 
8. Cook at 400Â° F until the upside of the lamb chops get a good color. 
9. Cook on other side until the other side has good color and is caramelized. Potatoes should be cooked through and lamb chops should be med well to well done.
