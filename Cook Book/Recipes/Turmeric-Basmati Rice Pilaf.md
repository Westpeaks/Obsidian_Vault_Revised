## Ingredients

- **1-2 tbsp** of olive oil or butter/ghee
- **1 ½ cups** of washed not soaked basmati rice
- **2-4 cloves** of garlic (sub powder if needed)
- ½ onion (sub powder if needed)
- **2 tsp** of salt
- **2tsp to 1 tbsp** of turmeric.
- **3 cups** of chicken stock, veg stock, or water
- **2** cardamom pods
- **2** bay leaves
- **1** cinnamon stick 

## Directions

1. Heat up pan then add oil.
2. If using raw garlic and onion (not powder), saute for 3-5 min. You add the onion first and saute as need to caramelize the onions. Then add the garlic in the last 3 minutes so that it doesn't burn. 
3. Add rice. Saute another 3-5 min.
4. Add salt and turmeric. Mix. 
5. Add stock, bay leaves, cinnamon stick, and cardamom.
6. Bring to a simmer and let simmer until the rice and stock are at the same level. 
7. Cover with lid and turn the burner off. 
8. Let steam for 15 minutes.
9. Serve and add black pepper and/or sumac. Lemon is also good.
