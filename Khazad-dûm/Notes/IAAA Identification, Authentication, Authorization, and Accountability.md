## Identification 

- Your name, username, ID number, employee number, SSN
- "I am Thor"

## Authentication

- "Prove that you are Thor" -- should always use MFA to do this. 
	- Something you know
	- Something you have 
	- Something you are (biometric scans)

## Authorization

- What are you allowed to access?
- We use access controls models to implement this (**Ex.** DAC, MAC, RBAC, RUBAC).

## Accountability

- The ability to trace an action to a subject identity.
- Prove that a user or process caused an action.
