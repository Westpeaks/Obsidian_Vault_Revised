# Engageli Interview Round 2 HR


### Types of Questions You Should Expect

1. **Behavioral Questions**:
    
    - **Examples**:
        - "**Can you describe a time when you had to manage a difficult client relationship?**"
		    - Issue at CMH.
		    - Issue at Mayo (bad reviews) or CHOP.
		    - Customers need to know you are taking care of them and that you are competent. This will make for a lot of issues and past treatment.
        - "**Tell me about a time when you went above and beyond to support a team member or customer.**"
	        - I am often advocating for customers internally. Working through difficult issues. Fighting for customer treatment. (CHOP, right now Mayo)
	        - Site down. Bad data. Bi-weekly meetings.
	        - Customers use tools to run a business. They are trying to work a job just like we are.  
    - **How to Answer**: Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) to structure your answers. Clearly outline the context, your responsibilities, the actions you took, and the outcomes of those actions.
      
2. **Technical and Role-Specific Questions**:
    
    - **Examples**:
        - "**How do you prioritize and manage multiple support requests from different partners?**"
	        - Priority is typically determined by customer buy-in.
	        - It is also determined from customer temperature (squeaky wheel).
	        - Individual tickets are determined by severity and impact. 
        - "**What experience do you have with educational technology platforms, and how do you support their users?**"
    - **How to Answer**: Highlight your organizational skills, familiarity with educational technology, and specific strategies you use for managing support requests, such as using ticketing systems or CRM tools.
      
3. **Cultural Fit Questions**:
    
    - **Examples**:
        - "**Why do you want to work at Engageli?**"
	        - It is a good platform and a good opportunity.
	        - Also, Matt seems like a great guy to work for. 
        - "**How do you handle working in a fast-paced, innovative environment?**"
    - **How to Answer**: Research Engageli’s mission, values, and recent developments. Show enthusiasm for their focus on education and innovation. Mention specific aspects of their culture or mission that resonate with you.
      
4. **Problem-Solving Questions**:
    
    - **Examples**:
        - "**What steps would you take if a partner reported a critical issue with the platform just before a major event?**"
	        - Assess the scope of the issue. Who will it affect? How extensive is it? Will it affect the event? 
	        - Assess who to involve to resolve the issue. Who are the key players?
	        - Come up with an action plan to resolve the issue.
	        - Identify the root cause. 
	        - Resolve the issue. 
        - "How do you ensure that partners are effectively onboarded and supported?"
    - **How to Answer**: Describe your problem-solving process, emphasizing your ability to stay calm under pressure, communicate effectively, and deploy technical and interpersonal skills to resolve issues promptly.

### How to Answer Them

1. **Be Specific and Structured**:
    
    - Use concrete examples from your past experience.
    - Structure your responses clearly, focusing on the actions you took and the results you achieved.
2. **Showcase Relevant Skills and Experience**:
    
    - Highlight your experience in customer or partner support, especially within the education or technology sectors.
    - Mention any relevant technical skills, such as familiarity with support software or educational platforms.
3. **Demonstrate Cultural Alignment**:
    
    - Show that you understand Engageli’s mission and values.
    - Convey enthusiasm for their work and how your personal and professional values align with theirs.

### How You Can Add to the Team

1. **Bring Relevant Experience**:
    
    - Highlight your background in customer support, education technology, or any roles where you’ve managed client relationships.
    - Mention specific achievements or improvements you’ve implemented in past roles.
2. **Showcase Your Skills**:
    
    - Emphasize skills like problem-solving, communication, and technical troubleshooting.
    - If you have experience with specific tools or platforms relevant to Engageli, be sure to mention them.
3. **Demonstrate a Proactive and Collaborative Approach**:
    
    - Explain how you’ve contributed to team success in the past through collaboration and proactive problem-solving.
    - Provide examples of how you’ve supported team members or improved team processes.
4. **Innovative Mindset**:
    
    - Share any ideas you have for improving partner support at Engageli.
    - Show that you are forward-thinking and willing to contribute to the company’s growth and innovation.

### Additional Tips

- **Research Engageli**: Understand their products, mission, recent news, and industry position. Tailor your answers to show you’ve done your homework.
- **Prepare Questions**: Have thoughtful questions ready about the team, company culture, and specific responsibilities of the role.
- **Follow-Up**: After the interview, send a thank-you email reiterating your interest in the role and highlighting a key point from your discussion.
