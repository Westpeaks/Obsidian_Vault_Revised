## Availability

### System Integrity and Data Availability

#### Threats:

- Malicious attacks (**Ex.** DDOS, physical, system compromise, staff).
- Application failures (errors in code).
- Component hardware (hardware failure).

### Methods of Mitigation:

- IPS/IDS.
- Patch Management
- Redundancy on power, disks, traffic paths (networking), HVAC, staff (no single point of failure), high availability measures. 
- SLAs - standard of uptime and other standards as a business practice.
