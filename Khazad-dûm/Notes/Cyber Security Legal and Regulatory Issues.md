## Legal and Regulatory Issues

### Criminal Law

- Crimes and law breaking based on a society or nation.
- Must be "Beyond a reasonable doubt."
- **Consequences** - Jail, death, and fines. 

### Civil Law

- Individuals, groups and organizations can have trails based on grievances. 
- Must be "the majority of proof."
- **Consequences** - Financial fines. 

### Administrative Law

- Laws enacted by government agencies (**Ex.** FDA, HIPAA, FAA).

### Private Regulations

- Compliance required by contract (**Ex.** PCI-DSS). 

### Customary Law

- To do with personal conduct and patterns of behavior. 
- It is founded on the traditions and customs of a particular area or region. 

### Religious Law

- Based on the religious beliefs of a particular area or country. They include a code of ethics which are required to be upheld. 

### Liability

Leadership is ultimately responsible for law infractions. 
- This does not mean that you will not be held liable. Diligence and due care vs. negligence.
