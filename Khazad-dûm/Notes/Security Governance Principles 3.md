## Security Governance

### Governance Standards and Control Frameworks

- **PCI-DSS** - Payment Card Industry Security Standard
	- It is a required standard to handle and issue debit and credit cards.
- **OCTAVE** - Operationally Critical Threat, Asset, and Vulnerability Evaluation
	- Practice of self directed risk management.
- **COBIT** - Control Objectives and information and related Technology.
	- The needs of stakeholders are mapped to IT goals (Operational). 
- **COSO** - Committee of Sponsoring Organization
	- Goals of the org.
- **ITIL** - Information Technology Infrastructure Library
- **FRAP** - Facilitated Risk Analysis Process. 
	- Analyzed one business unit, application of system at a time in internal brainstorm and discussion. Impact is analyzed. Threats and risk is determined and prioritized.
