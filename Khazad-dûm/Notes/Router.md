## Routes traffic between IP subnets

- Routers are an OSI layer 3 device. 
- There are certain cases where a router might be built into a switch. In these cases they can be called **Layer 3 switches**.
	- The switch exists on layer 2 (Data link). 
	- The Router exists on Layer 3 (Network). 
- Routers connect different types of networks together. 
	- **Ex.** LAN, WAN, copper, fiber
