## Intellectual Property

### Copyright

- Ownership of what you create. 
- You do not need to apply for this. It is automatic.
- Lasts for 70 years after creator's death. 95 years for corps. 

**Attack:** Piracy, Infringement 

### Trademarks

- Trademarks are all the branding associated with a product. 
- Must be registered. 
- Good for 10 years. 

**Attack:** Counterfeiting (product fakes)

### Patents

- Protects inventions. 
- Lasts 20 years. 

**Attack:** Infringement

### Trade Secrets

- Owned by a company or individual and private. 
- If leaked, there is no protection. 

**Attack:** No recourse, except if the secrets are gained by illegal means.
