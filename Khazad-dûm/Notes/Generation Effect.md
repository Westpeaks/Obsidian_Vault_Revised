## Definition

- The amount of effort and time combined it takes to start actively learning a topic. 
- The more effort that is put into learning the less time it will take to learn a topic. You have to engage in struggle to get your brain to comprehend learning. The effectiveness of this struggle is the **generation effect**.

### Ask yourself

- Am I reaching the right level struggle?
- As you are reading pick out which ideas seem to be more inportant than other ideas. Summarize those. 
- This will initiate the generation effect. 

## When writing notes

**Consider the following when writing notes**

- What to write.
- How to write it.
- How to express it. 
- Which things are more important.
