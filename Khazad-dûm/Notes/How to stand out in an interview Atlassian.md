# How to stand out in an interview

1. Send thank you
2. Follow up 
3. Research interviewers
4. Ask questions
5. State your intentions

##### ==When faced with technical skills questions, think about how you have done this before and how you can apply those skills. Connect it to something similar. Give yourself time to think.==   

How I meet the role at Atlassian:

I work with SQL currently at my job. I have extensive conceptual experience of relational databases. 

I have gained a great amount of Linux command line knowledge. I have spent a significant amount of hours using command line.

I have worked extensively with REST API's. Developed projects and have a lot of experience troubleshooting API's. 

I write and understand scripts at my current job. Proficient in coding in python. 

I have extensive experience working with and troubleshooting SSO.  

Expertise to write and update SQL queries with ease. Writing a SQL query is a lot like writing a script. I write and update queries at my current job. 

Monica works in DFW. You grew up there.
