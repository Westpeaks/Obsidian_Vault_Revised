## Bridging done on hardware

- Engineered as an **Application-Specific Integrated Circuit** (ASIC).

### An OSI Layer 2 Device (Data Link)

- Switches forward traffic based on data link addresses. 

### Contain many ports and features
- Switches are often considered the core of an enterprise network. 
- Many switches also can provide **Power over Ethernet** (PoE).
